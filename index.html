Our story

A little corner of warmth in the heart of the city

Golden Crumb began with a simple dream: a place where the smell of fresh bread greets you at the door and every cup is poured with care. Our head baker, Elena, trained in Paris; our coffee comes from small farms in Colombia and Ethiopia.

We believe great food doesn't need to be complicated — just honest ingredients, patience, and a whole lot of butter. Whether you're grabbing a croissant on the run or settling in with a slice of cake, you're family here.

  • 🌱 Locally sourced whenever possible
  • 🧈 European-style laminated pastries
  • ♻️ Compostable cups & packaging
